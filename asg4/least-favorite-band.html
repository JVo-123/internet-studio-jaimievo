Least Favorite Band: BTS

Why?

They aren't the worst ever, and it's not that I don't like them. I just don't listen to their music, but I will jam out a little to their songs if it's playing in the mall or something. The reason why They're my least favorite is because:

  1. I know a fair amount of their songs to give them a fair chance at being one of my favorites.
  2. There are just groups that I prefer more than them.

I can't deny that they have talent; there's a reason why they're still the most well-know K-pop boy band. I just prefer listening to songs from other groups and artists more than them.
