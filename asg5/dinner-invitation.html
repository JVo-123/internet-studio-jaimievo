Dinner Invitation

Guests

My guests are from various dreams I've had.

Lonely Bird

I stumbled upon an animal sanctuary at school, which is where I met Lonely Bird. And I'm not just calling it that by the way; other students and staff just called it that. I heard about its story of why it's lonely, but I didn't remember anything, so I couldn't write it down. I hope it's made friends since I've seen it, so I'd like to catch up with it.

Fire Lady

After leaving the animal sanctuary, I saw her really distraught at some other staff while they were having a meeting on the track field. She was the school nurse with healing fire powers. I don't know how to describe how the fire felt other than it feeling like waving my hand through slightly warmer than room-temperature smoke from a smoke machine. I remember her being very beautiful, so I want to see her again. I also never got to know her name, so I'd like to know.

Fancy Lady

To be honest, my interaction with her was the most vague compared to the others. Her dream was such a blur, I couldn't write anything about it when I woke up. She wasn't from the same dream as Lonely Bird and the Fire Lady, which also makes it too difficult to know what she did exactly. I just remember she had a really cool dress with frilly stuff that stood up and covered the bottom part of her face sometimes. I also remember her being very beautiful and nameless, so I'd like to see her again and learn her name.
