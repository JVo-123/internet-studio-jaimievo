Welcome to Pelican Town!

Introduction

Welcome to Pelican Town, a nice getaway (and potential home) near the beachside of Stardew Valley, far from the hustle and bustle of the city. There are a ton of local businesses where you can purchase fresh produce, furniture, supplies, and lots more! Here in Pelican Town, there is a large emphasis on community with loads of events that take place each season, from the Egg Hunt to the Feast of the Winter Star. There’s also a forest, mine, quarry, desert, and island if you’re feeling adventurous. We’re looking forward to seeing you!

Highlights/Factoids:

  • The valley can sometimes have green rain, but don’t worry, it’s harmless!
  • A Pelican Town tradition for proposing is giving a partner a Mermaid Pendant!
  • The year consists of 28-day seasons!
  • Ignore the large bush northwest to the bridge leading to the beach at 12:40am.
  • The bushes grow either blackberries or salmonberries depending on the season!
