Desert Festival

On Spring 15th to 17th, everyone rides the bus to Calico Desert to participate in various activities, like customizing exquisite dishes, having Emily create an outfit for you each day, and receiving a completely unique free cactus! You can also participate in challenges like fishing, betting, and entering the Skull Cavern to earn currency specific to the event: Calico Eggs. With the Calico Eggs, you can purchase various items from the stands.

Stardew Valley Fair

This event takes place on Fall 16th in the center of town! Here the town gathers to play fishing, target-shooting, and other carnival games to earn points and win prizes. You can also have your future told by a very talented fortuneteller that's scarily accurate! The main part of this fair are the displays locals set up to present their produce and products to be judged by Mayor Lewis himself. The winner is given 1,000 points and bragging rights, so it's worth giving it a try!

Night Market

From Winter 15th to 17th, an on-boat market opens from 5pm to 2am at the beach! There are merchants that sell art and all sorts of miscellaneous items that are aften seasonal. The event also has a mermaid show and submarine that allows you to catch creatures from deep underwater. Also, there's free coffee!
